Meet me

I've got a master's degree and teaching degree in Musical Composition. I'm a teacher in the professional sound area, music and communication. Currently, I'm focusing in web development: I've learned React, Angular, Node and Express, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git and GitHub.
My relationship with computer science has began in music studies. These artistic and academic beginnings have made me merge my study research in an interdisciplinary way, from exacts sciences to social sciences. Likewise, my college studies made me work in algorithm composition with Csound, a C language based program. Thereby, my professional work is grounded on cross-functional subjects. In this manner, I approach new challenges with pasion, seriousness, ﬂexibility, multidisciplinarity and curiousness.

Web Development

Intrigued by the intersection of music and technology, my computer science journey began during my music studies with Csound. Papers

In my repository of musical papers you will find a curated collection that delves into the intricacies of music composition and analysis. Within these pages, you will encounter a diverse array of writings dedicated to the art and science of music. This collection also includes analyses of music by Piazzolla and Jorge Horst, as well as examinations of my own compositions. Furthermore, it engages in lively discussions spanning semiotics, aesthetics, philosophy and psychoanalisys.
